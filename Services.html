Crankshaft Grinder

Our acclaimed Crank Shaft Grinder has a regrinding length up 4575 mm for any type of D.G.Sets, marine engines and compressors including straightening of bend crankshaft.

After extending the services of crankshaft grinding and crankshaft polishing to the industry, our Company has introduced portable, light weight, easy to operate onsite crankshaft grinding machine which can be carried to the site to execute crankshaft grinding, crankshaft repair and crankshaft polishing without dismantling the crankshaft from the block.
