Insitu Boring / Line Boring

Line boring is a special type of in-situ boring operation for machining two or more bores in a straight line. The line boring machine is carried out in-situ with our specially designed in-situ line boring machine that is capable of achieving high degree of accuracy and surface finish.

We provide service for Line boring of main housing & main bearing to maintain axis alignment of housing to its original dimension far D.G. Sets, Marine Engines, Compressors, Gear Boxes etc. Length up to 7500 mm and housing dia upto 600 mm as well as pricision bedding and alignment of big crankshaft on main bearing in bedplate to achieve wed deflection readings within permissible limits. We also specialise in INSITU line boring jobs at any location in the country.
